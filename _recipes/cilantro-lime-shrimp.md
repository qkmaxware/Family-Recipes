    amount: 1 tsp.
    notes: 
    group: Main Dish

## Instructions
1.	add oil and garlic in a large saucepan. Heat to medium/high
2.	add in shrimp and sautee for 5-10 minutes until shrimp is pink
3.	Squeeze on lime juice, season with salt, pepper, cumin and cilantro
