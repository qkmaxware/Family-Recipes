---
title: Banana Bread
categories:
- loafs
- breads
ingredients:
  butter: 2 tbsp
  sugar: 1 cup
  eggs: 2
  nuts: 1/2 cup, optional
  baking soda: 1 tsp
  water: 1 tbsp
  flour: 1 1/2 cup
  bananas: 2-3 ripe, mashed
---
## Instructions:
1.	Cream butter, sugar and eggs together
2.	Dissolve baking soda in water. Add to the mixture.
3.	Add flours, bananas and nuts
4.	Bake 50-60 minutes in a greased loaf pan at 350 degrees
